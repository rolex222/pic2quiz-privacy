Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Pic 2 Quiz. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application. By using Pic 2 Quiz, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using your Microsoft account, we receive your name, email address, and profile information provided by Microsoft through their authentication service (Microsoft Entra ID).
  • Camera & Photos: With your permission, the app accesses your device camera and photo library to allow you to capture or select images for quiz creation. Images are processed to generate quiz content and may be uploaded to our servers.
  • User-Generated Content: Quiz sets, answers, community posts, and other content you create within the app are stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app (e.g., features used, session duration) to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as operating system version and device model may be collected for compatibility and support purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and manage your account
  • Process images and generate quiz content using AI services
  • Enable community features such as sharing and participating in quiz sets
  • Improve app performance, features, and user experience
  • Respond to support requests or inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

Third-Party Services

Pic 2 Quiz uses the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Microsoft Entra ID (Azure AD): Used for secure authentication. Governed by Microsoft's Privacy Statement.
  • Microsoft Azure: Our backend infrastructure and AI services run on Microsoft Azure cloud. Data is stored and processed in Azure data centers.

Camera Permission

The app requests access to your device camera solely to allow you to take photos for quiz creation. We do not access the camera in the background, and camera usage is always initiated by your explicit action within the app.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Microsoft Azure cloud servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and user-generated content for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Children's Privacy

Pic 2 Quiz is designed for users of all ages, including young children. The app's content and interface are intentionally child-friendly and suitable for use by children as young as 6 years old, under parental supervision.

Because our app is directed at children, we take their privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable children's privacy laws.

For children under the age of 13, we require the following:

  • Parental Consent: A parent or legal guardian must provide verifiable consent before a child under 13 creates an account or uses features that involve data collection (such as Microsoft sign-in or image uploads).
  • Parental Supervision: We strongly encourage parents and guardians to supervise their child's use of the app.
  • Limited Data Collection: We only collect information from children that is necessary to operate the app's core features.
  • Parental Rights: Parents or guardians may review, request correction of, or request deletion of their child's personal data at any time by contacting us.

If you believe that a child has provided personal information without parental consent, please contact us immediately at reviewmasterai@gmail.com and we will promptly remove such information.
