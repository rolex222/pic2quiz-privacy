Account Deletion

Delete Your Account

You have the right to delete your Pic 2 Quiz account and all associated personal data at any time. You can do this directly through the app or by contacting us via email.

How to Delete Your Account

Option 1 — Recommended

🗑️ Delete Directly in the App

The fastest and easiest way to delete your account is directly inside the Pic 2 Quiz app:

  1. Open the Pic 2 Quiz app
  2. Tap the Profile / User icon in the top navigation bar
  3. Tap "User Settings"
  4. Scroll down to the Danger Zone section
  5. Tap "Delete My Account"
  6. Read the warning and tap "Yes, Delete My Account" to confirm

Your account and all associated data will be immediately and permanently deleted upon confirmation.

Option 2 — Via Email

✉️ Request Deletion by Email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us:

  1. Send an email to reviewmasterai@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the email address associated with your Pic 2 Quiz account
  4. We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm via email

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed:

Data Type Action
Account information (name, email) from Microsoft sign-in Permanently deleted
Quiz sets you created (not shared to any community) Permanently deleted
Quiz sets you shared to communities Anonymized — creator shown as "Deleted User", content kept for community members
Communities you own (including all their members, posts & quizzes) Permanently deleted
Community memberships (communities you joined) Permanently deleted
Posts and messages you sent in all communities Permanently deleted
Token usage history and purchased token balance Permanently deleted
Photos and images uploaded for quiz generation Not stored — images are processed and immediately discarded
⚠️ This action is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, your data cannot be recovered. Please make sure you want to permanently delete your account before proceeding.
